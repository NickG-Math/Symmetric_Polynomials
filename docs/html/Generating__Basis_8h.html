Detailed Description

If R=Q[x_1,...,x_n,y_1,...,y_n]/(y_i^2=y_i), the goal is to produce algebra generators for the fixed points of R under the Sigma_n action (permuting the x_i,y_i separately), give an algorithm for writing a fixed point in terms of the generators and an algorithm for producing the relations of those generators.
